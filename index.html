Fortnite Battle Royale Battle Pass

Fortnite Battle Royale, published by Epic Games, is a free online video game that took the world by storm. The game consists of 100 players landing in a map and once you land your objective is to survive untill only one team is left. To acomplish a Victory Royale you will need to collect materials,loot, build, and show great skill. This fan site will show you the character skins you can obtain if you buy the Battle Pass.

What is a Battle Pass?

In Fortnite every new season comes with a new Battle Pass where you can make progression and unlock new items.You have to buy the pass for 950 vbucks(aprox. 9.50 dlls) to be able to collect all available rewards and access challenges. The Battle Pass allows you to earn enough vbucks to unlock the upcoming season battle pass if not spent in other items in the Fortnite shop.

What do colors mean?

Colors in fortnite signify the rarity of an item and also dictates the prices of skins when they appear in the shop.

  1. Legendary
  2. Epic
  3. Rare
  4. Uncommon
